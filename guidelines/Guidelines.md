# Companion App - Design & Development Guidelines

## Brand Voice
- First-person warm voice from the AI ("I remember...", "I've noticed...")
- Use "companions" not "providers" or "freelancers"
- Use "connections" not "projects" or "engagements"
- Use "sessions" not "bookings" or "appointments"
- Tone: warm, genuine, trust-oriented, emotionally intelligent

## Visual Design System
- Background: #FBF9F7 (warm neutral)
- Content panels: White with #E8E4DF borders
- Primary gradient: rose-500 to amber-500
- Rounded corners: 24px (rounded-3xl)
- Typography: Inter font family
- Large real portrait avatars (circular)
- Minimal UI elements, generous whitespace

## Color Palette
- Warm accent: from-rose-400 to-amber-400 gradient
- Trust/verified: green-500
- Rating: amber-400
- Backgrounds: #F7F5F2 for secondary surfaces
- Text: gray-900 primary, gray-400 secondary, gray-500 body

## Key Principles
- Human, not corporate
- Trust-based, not transactional
- Simple, not feature-heavy
- Premium, not utilitarian
- Emotionally intelligent, not data-driven feeling
